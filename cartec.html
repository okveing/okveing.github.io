Carcano® Cartec®

For over half a century the name Stamperia CARCANO® has been intertwined with that of metal forging: an Italian family tradition which is still, even today, their company’s business card throughout the world. 2007 saw the launch of the Cartec® range of lifting fittings, a name now synonymous with quality hooks, links & lifting points

With their specialisation in the lifting & lashing industry, Unilift are proud to offer a complete range of Cartec® products including weld eyes & bolt-on lifting points, hooks & shortening clutches.
