When he originally founded Unilift in 2001, it was initially established as a ‘second string in the bow’ for his existing family plumbing & roofing business. But within two years, it was clear that Unilift was destined to be more more than just a side-line. Edwin has been a key player in this successful growth, leading Unilift through both the tough times and the times of expansion, establishing Unilift as one of the major players in the heavy-lift industry. With a passion for excellence and customer satisfaction, it is not uncommon to still see Edwin on the factory floor helping the wire rope workers or warehouse team, or running urgent deliveries across the country</p>
