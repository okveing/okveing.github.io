Wire Rope - General Purpose

There are many situations where a simple, solid & dependable wire rope is all that is required.

General purpose or lower usage applications, overhaul transition periods or re-sale fitment – all of these situations may call for a good quality wire rope but may not require the exceptional capabilities of our Unilift® or DIEPA® range of speciality wire ropes. In these situations we offer a range of solid, general-purpose ropes from a variety of rope manufacturers. Please contact us to discuss with one of our representatives which wire rope will best suit your current application.

Applications include:

  • Lifting
  • Marine
  • Construction
  • Structural
  • Aerial ropeways
  • Mining
  • Oil & Gas
  • Structural strands
  • Stackers & reclaimers
