Engineered lifting systems

Specialised applications require specialised systems. Over the years we have seen first hand the sheer scope of uses standard lifting products can be put to.

But we also recognise that there are many situations (governed by size or shape, access, environment or complexity) where off-the-shelf products simply cannot achieve a satisfactory result. So to be able to provide the high level of service that our customers have come to expect, at Unilift have steadily built up a design, engineering, manufacturing and testing department capable of producing a complete range of custom fabricated lifting beams, end caps, modular spreader beams and complete modular engineered systems.

All our products are designed & manufactured to meet AS4100 & AS4991 standards. Alternative international standards such as DNV can also be facilitated upon customer request. We can also supply a full range of procedural & compliancy documentation to suit the most stringent corporate systems.

Unilift Lifting Beams

Whether in the factory, around the warehouse or out on the docks, fabricated lifting beams are the go-to lifting product in many industries. As they are often used as a permanent or semi-permanent solution for many repetitive lifting tasks, making sure that the lifting beam is designed to be ‘just right’ for the job at hand is an important part of the selection process. Unilift have designed & manufactured a wide range of lifting beams. So whether you are after a standard lifting beam of a certain length or a custom beam for a particular lift, we have both the design, engineering & manufacturing capability to meet all your lift & safety requirements.

Unilift End Caps

End caps are a highly versatile lifting product. Easy to store and maintain, they can be easily transferred between various lengths of pipe to suit each individual lifting situation. With a great deal of input from our customers, Unilift have developed a truly world leading end cap design. Versatile and easily assembled, the unique engineered shape of our end caps greatly reduces the sole reliance upon the integrity of the welds to give them their superior strength. This has allowed us to produce a lighter, stronger and more streamlined end cap, one that can be manufactured for lifts of up to 3000 metric tonnes.

Unilift Modular & Adjustable Spreader Beams

Over the years, Unilift have worked closely with clients who require a modular spreader beam that is cost effective, easy to use and fast to assemble. The Unilift modular spreader beam system fulfils all these requirements, and is fast becoming a favourite of engineers throughout Australasia. Available in capacities to 3000 tonne and with a lift spread of up to 50 metres, our beams are proven, tested, tough & true.

Unilift Engineered Modular Spreader Systems

Unilift engineered modular spreader systems are becoming some of the most advanced lifting solutions ever seen. Our design and engineering team are working extensively with clients in the oil & gas sector to develop lifting systems that will enable the large scale lifts – of sometimes fragile components – to take place with absolute precision & safety. The capacity of Unilift engineered modular spreader systems are virtually unlimited, and are provided as a complete package which includes all the required cable and hardware to accomplish the lift successfully. Our rigging division manufactures suitable SWR cable laid slings & grommets in-house, and will complement these with products from our extensive range of shackles, hooks, anchors and synthetic high performance slings..
