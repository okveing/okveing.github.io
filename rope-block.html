RopeBlock®

Ropeblock® hardware is found worldwide wherever key components in crane systems are in contact with the wire rope. Their products are designed to contribute to consistent safety and efficiency in any type of lifting & rigging application. RopeBlock® have extensive knowledge of blocks, sockets, and rigging gear: they manufacture and globally distribute thousands of blocks and tens of thousands of sheaves and sockets a year.

With their specialisation in the extreme heavy-lift crane industry, Unilift are proud to offer a complete range of heavy-lift RopeBlock® products including sockets, blocks, sheaves & turn-buckles.
