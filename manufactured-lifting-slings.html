Lifting Slings - Chain, wire-rope & synthetic

The humble lifting sling is one of the most common & versatile lifting products in the industry. But do your current slings best suit your application?

One-leg to five-leg chain slings, adjustable slings, round slings, single laid rope slings, cable laid slings & grommets, adjustable DNV deep sea slings and light duty polyester slings all play their part in the lifting industry. So contact one of our representatives to discuss which sling is best for your application.

Chain Slings

Constructed from quality components supplied by manufacturers such as PWB Anchor, Gunnebo, Cartec, Yoke, Crosby, GN Rope & Green Pin, Unilift chain slings are designed for purpose and built to last.

Unilift Chain Sling Range

Single-laid Wire Rope Slings

From small transport or crane slings to large shipping or off-shore slings, Unilift can manufacture a full range of hard eye, soft-eye or socketed slings to suit every lifting requirement. Hand spliced using our range of premium quality wire ropes, you can be certain that your Unilift slings will stand the test of time.

Applications

Cable laid slings & grommets

With most of our customers in the heavy-lift or offshore industries, precision, reliability and safety are paramount. Using quality wire ropes as our starting point, Unilift cable laid slings & grommets are manufactured, checked, measured and certified to IMCA & M179 standards. When the conditions are at their toughest, you can be sure our cable laid slings & grommets will not let you down.

Applications

Unilift High-Performance Deep-Sea Slings

Unilift have designed and developed specialised synthetic slings for heavy lift in the offshore industry and subsea applications. These slings are engineered to suit each customers’s Critical Lift Plan. Unilift slings come with extra protection in the eye and are designed to suit hooks and shackles where small bend radius is an issue. All slings can be approved and tested as per DNV requirements. Slings up to a minimum breaking load (MBL) of 4300 metric tonnes are available with a comprehensive certification package including description, calculations, drawings and all certificates.

Applications

Unilift Deepsea Adjustable Slings

Some lifts can more complicated than others, especially when the lifted object is very delicate. The lifting & lowering of jumper oil well connections is a good example of this type of lift. Multiple slings are attached to a number of lifting points and the sling lengths all need to be optimised to avoid straining or twisting the jumper. Making small adjustments during setup to this style of lift when using steel slings, chains & shackles can be a very time consuming process, ranging from hours to days. But there is an alternative.

Unilift adjustable slings are pre-spliced synthetic rope slings that are soft, light and easy to work with. While manufactured to the optimal calculated length for the required lift, if minor adjustments in sling length are required to maintain optimal tension, the length of the sling can be shortened or lengthened in only a few minutes, with no tools required. These slings can be supplied with either soft-eyes and a protective sleeve, or steel thimbles, depending upon project specifications.

Applications

DNV Certified Offshore Sling Assemblies

The offshore environment can throw up some of the most dangerous and damaging lifting conditions on earth. Extreme sub-zero temperatures, salt water, heavy seas and high wind not only impact directly on the lifting conditions, but also greatly increase the wear & tear on the equipment.

Into the fray comes Unilift’s DNV certified offshore sling assemblies. Designed specifically for offshore container usage and available in 4 or 5 leg variations (without forerunners), our offshore sling assemblies utilise the highest quality steel rope, and are supplied with premium quality fittings from suppliers such as Gunnebo, Yoke, or GreenPin. Whatever Mother Nature can throw up – we can tackle head on!

Applications

Polyester Round Slings

Unilift round slings are manufactured from 100% polyester and are most commonly used in the lifting industry for light to medium lifts. Our slings range from 10 tonne to 150 tonne in capacity and are manufactured with a heavy duty cover to ensure longer life and excellent protection of the load bearing yarn. Slings in this range are all available at relatively short notice.
