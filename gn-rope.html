GN Rope Fittings®

GN Rope Fittings® was founded in the Netherlands in 1923 as a manufacturer for small tools. Nowadays GN is a leading designer and manufacturer of forged products for mooring, heavy lifting, anchor handling and rigging applications.

With their specialisation in the extreme heavy-lift industry, Unilift are proud to offer a complete range of heavy-lift GN Rope Fittings® products including shackles, thimbles and sockets for the energy & offshore sector.

Stocked GN Rope Fittings® products include:

GN® Shackles

Including: Wide-body Shackle
— Shackles are used in lifting and static systems as removable links to connect (steel) wire rope, chain and other fittings…

GN® Thimbles

GN® Sockets
