Other Services

Proof Load Testing

Unilift has always been known for its technical support with testing and inspection of lifting and rigging equipment. We offer highly specialised service for both in-house and on-site testing. Staff are trained and experienced for all types of proof load testing in accordance to Australian Standards and International Standards. Testing services offered include:

  1. Proof Load
  2. Destruction Testing
  3. Cyclic
  4. Test Weights

Inspections

Unilift carry out on-site lifting and rigging inspection surveys for all industries who use lifting, rigging, mooring and working at heights equipment. All equipment is inspected to relevant Australian Standards, or the manufacturers guidelines. All equipment is entered into our database showing its identification number, WLL, certificate number and the full description of the gear.

Wire Rope Services

Unilift’s experienced personnel are highly skilled in the methods and procedures for handling, socketing and installation of steel wire ropes and steel cable systems. Technicians are experienced with spooling of ropes on-shore and off-shore and socketing. All other related services consists of:

  1. Electromagnetic NDT inspection
  2. Spooling
  3. Wire rope analysis and recommendations
  4. Long splicing
  5. Pressure lubrication
  6. Load testing
  7. Destructive testing
  8. Service life solution
  9. Problem solving and trouble-shooting premature rope failure
  10. Storage

Design & Manufacture

Unilift fabrication services brings together engineering and manufacturing expertise to provide a wide range of custom spreader bars and lifting attachments, fully tested and certified. Engineers have called upon Unilift to assist with design and manufacture of Spreader Bar and Rigging systems for the most complex lifting projects in the country. We always come up with a solution with our vast experience. Products we manufacture include:
