General Lifting Hardware

While custom slings & engineered lifting systems are our speciality, Unilift also carry a complete range of quality lifting & lashing hardware in a range of sizes & grades from suppliers such as Gunnebo, Stamperia, Yoke, GreenPin and Crosby.

Chain slings, shackles, blocks & sheaves, winches, load cells and anchor points are all off-the-shelf items and are available at short notice to to fit out your crane, engineering workshop, rig or vessel.

Hooks, Links & Chain Slings

For all your general crane handling needs, we carry a full range of 1, 2, 3 & 4 leg chain slings, hooks, links and anchors from Stamperia, PWB Anchor & Gunnebo.

Shackles

A staple product in the lifting arsenal, the humble shackle is in fact one of the most important links in every lift! Screw pin, round pin, bolt type, wide body, bow, sling, clevis, polar rated and Subsea ROV shackles all play their part. Unilift can offer you a full range of shackles from some of the best suppliers in the industry.
