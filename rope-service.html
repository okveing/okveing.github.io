Wire Rope Services

Unilift’s experienced personnel are highly skilled in both the new and traditional methods & procedures for handling, socketing and installation of steel wire ropes, steel cable systems and synthetic ropes. Our technicians are also experienced with the mainenance, installation and spooling of ropes on-shore and off-shore. Other related rope services include:

  1. Electromagnetic NDT inspection
  2. Spooling
  3. Wire rope analysis and recommendations
  4. Long splicing
  5. Pressure lubrication
  6. Load testing
  7. Destructive testing
  8. Service life solution
  9. Problem solving and trouble-shooting premature rope failure
  10. Storage
