Gunnebo® Industries

Gunnebo® Industries are an international company with manufacturing hubs in the USA, Sweden & Norway. They are global leaders in several areas within the lifting & material handling industry, most notably crane blocks, wire rope sheaves, chain & lifting components, shackles and lashing products.

Unilift are proud to offer a complete range of Gunnebo® product including a wide range of Grade 100 lifting & lashing chain fittings and hardware.
